The Power of Money
Photograph
The photograph is intended to communicate that money creates divisions within a society on a local and international scale. I wanted to make the viewer think about how money impacts their societies as some could argue that it does, indeed, buy power. It is also a typography experiment, as I explored creating a metaphor using physical objects to create the lettering.
