Sea Side
Photography & Collage
Victoria BC is known for being one of the most beautiful and lush islands in Canada. Exploring its natural environment, I went to various beaches and photographed objects such as rocks, shells and grass. I then brought the images into Photoshop and composed a digital collage of what I had found. I wanted the piece to reflect the various textures found within the objects and their ecosystem. The result was an abstract collage intended to make the viewer reflect on how they perceive and interact with nature.
