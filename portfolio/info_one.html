Typeset & Layout Design
For this project, I created a mock brochure for an art gallery. Through creating the brochure, I experimented with various layouts. Using a grid, I developed a collection of spreads that are each unique yet cohesive. I created a visual system intended to feel playful and modern that compliments the variety of artwork featured in the brochure. I used this project as an opportunity to play around with typesetting, exploring how to properly format the headers and body text. Overall, I learnt a lot about controlling visual hierarchy in print media.
