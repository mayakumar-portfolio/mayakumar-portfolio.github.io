The Zyeloa Project
Abstraction Series
The Zyeloa project is an ongoing series of abstractions that I began back in March 2020. I use it as a creative outlet to experiment with visual hierarchy, textures and abstraction. The compositions are created using a combination of photography, drawing and digital manipulation. I aim to use both physical and digital tools to build multi-dimensional images. I created an Instagram page for the project to further explore how to utilise social media for artwork and branding.
