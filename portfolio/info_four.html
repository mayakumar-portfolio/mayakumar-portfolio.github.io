Typeset & Layout Design
Using the table of contents from a photography book, I re-designed the layout to be visually captivating. I wanted to keep the design simple, so I built the design off of lines. I used the lines to create an array of boxes to hold the various sizes of information for each chapter. I controlled the visual hierarchy to ensure that the reader could easily find the information they wanted, despite the unconventional layout.
