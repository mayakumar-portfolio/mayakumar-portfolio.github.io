Get To Know Me

Maya is a graphic designer based in Canada.

I discovered my passion for graphic design when I was 13 and since then I’ve been playing with shapes, textures and colours. I am now working towards a bachelor’s degree in design at York University and just completed my first year. While in the program, I have taken studio courses in typography, visual communication, UX design, drawing and form. Having been familiar with the software prior to university, I can now confidently say that I am well versed in numerous Adobe applications such as InDesign, Illustrator and Photoshop. I am constantly exploring new styles and techniques inspired by digital & physical artwork to ensure that I am pushing myself as a designer. Ultimately, I strive to create sophisticated designs that are both visually engaging and functional.
